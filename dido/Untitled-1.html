Mailfy – Shopify Email Notification Templates

A modern, unique and minimal design for Shopify Store, brand, portfolio, and blogs. Suitable for any kind of corporate, office, start-up, agency, business, and general categories.

10 Unique Modules compatible with Stampready, Mailchimp and Campaign Monitor builder. Supports background images in all major email clients including outlook. Carefully crafted layouts are suitable to build fully responsive email campaign for Outlook, Gmail, Apple mail, Yahoo, Thunderbird, Android, AOL mail and much more.
